What we’re working on?

A technological solution that can accurately perform the Automatic Number Plate Recognition (ANPR) along with Facial Recognition from the available CCTV feeds. The solution should be able to recognize number plates that are written in typical non-standard ways using varying font styles, sizes, designs, symbols, languages etc., i.e. difficult to recognize by existing ANPR Systems.

View Product Prototype
What are our Use Cases?
  • Automation: The automated recognition of number plates allows automated alerts and controls for facilities. Hence, ANPR is a key technology for smart cities or toll stations.
  • Law enforcement: Police forces use ANPR for law enforcement purposes, to check if a vehicle is registered, or to identify vehicles related to traffic violations. The ability to detect and recognize number plates in real-time allows authorities to identify vehicles and track their location. Along with this police can also detect that the driver is above 18 or not or can also detect the faces of those people who have the criminal profiles.
  • Private Security: Private security companies can use this solution to monitor the premises of private properties such as office buildings, residential complexes, and shopping centers allowing for accurate tracking of visitors and potential security threats
  • Border Control: This solution can be used at border crossings to identify vehicles and drivers entering and leaving the country allowing for accurate tracking of border crossings and identification of potential security threats.
  • Parking Management: This solution can be used in parking lots and garages to manage parking and monitor vehicles entering and leaving the premises allowing for accurate billing and tracking of parked vehicles.

Overall, ANPR and FRS solutions have a wide range of applications across industries and sectors, and are becoming increasingly important for ensuring security, efficiency, and convenience.
